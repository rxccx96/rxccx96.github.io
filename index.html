About

I’m a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Bayreuth working at the intersection of vegetation ecology and conservation science. My research focuses on biodiversity patterns, protected areas effectiveness (including Natura 2000), and vegetation dynamics across European landscapes, with particular emphasis on mountain and Mediterranean ecosystems.
