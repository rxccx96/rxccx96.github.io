About

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Bayreuth, working at the intersection of vegetation ecology and conservation science. My research explores how plant communities respond to environmental change, human pressure, and conservation strategies across Europe.

I am particularly interested in mountain and Mediterranean ecosystems, where climate change, land-use history, and biodiversity conservation interact in complex ways. Through fieldwork, spatial analyses, and large-scale ecological data, my goal is to support evidence-based conservation and biodiversity planning.

Research interests

  • Plant diversity and biogeography
  • Protected areas and Natura 2000 effectiveness
  • Vegetation dynamics and disturbance ecology
  • Mountain and Mediterranean ecosystems
  • Macroecology and conservation planning
